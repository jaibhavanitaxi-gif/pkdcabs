About Tipeshwar Wildlife Sanctuary

Tipeshwar Wildlife Sanctuary is a 148.63 sq km protected forest located near Pandharkawada in Yavatmal district, Maharashtra. Often called Maharashtra's hidden gem, it is one of the best places in India to spot Bengal Tigers in the wild.

The sanctuary is home to over 20 tigers (up from just 3 in 2010), making it a remarkable conservation success story. With lush teak forests, bamboo groves, and diverse wildlife, Tipeshwar offers an intimate and uncrowded safari experience compared to famous parks like Tadoba.
